Uniform Health provides military spouses and dependants enrolled in Tricare to better manage their healthcare. Easily view and schedule appointments. And manage all your referrals in one app.


At Uniform Health, our mission is to empower military personnel and their families by simplifying the process of scheduling and managing healthcare referrals and appointments. We understand the unique challenges you face, and we're dedicated to providing a seamless and efficient experience, ensuring your well-deserved care is always within reach.

Features

Request and manage referrals directly in the Uniform Health App.


View all your appointments across locations fore each dependant in the family.


Schedule or cancel an appointment anytime with your primary care doctor, or approval referral.
